Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! We offer a free pilot where we source 50 candidates for one role at no cost. Start your free pilot →

What's a "qualified candidate"?

A qualified candidate matches your ideal profile criteria and has been scored 70+ by our AI. We only count candidates that actually fit your requirements.

Can I change plans anytime?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. No lock-in contracts.

How does billing work?

We bill monthly via credit card through Stripe. You can cancel anytime and maintain access until the end of your billing period.

Do unused candidates roll over?

Unused candidate quota does not roll over month-to-month. However, we can discuss custom arrangements for Agency plans.

What if I need more candidates?

You can upgrade to a higher plan anytime, or contact us about overage rates for one-time spikes in hiring.
