What's included in the free pilot?
We'll source 50 candidates for your hardest-to-fill role, send personalized outreach, and deliver interested candidates to your inbox. No credit card required, no commitment. If you love it, we'll talk about a paid plan.
How is this different from other recruiting tools?
Most tools give you a database to search or templates to send. HireLane is a done-for-you service powered by AI. We handle everything from sourcing to outreach to screening. You just talk to interested candidates.
What kinds of roles does this work for?
We work best for technical roles (engineering, product, design) and specialized positions where finding qualified candidates is hard. If you're filling entry-level or high-volume roles, we might not be the best fit.
Do candidates know it's AI?
The outreach is personalized and human-sounding. Candidates respond to a real email address and eventually talk to a real person (you). We're transparent that technology assists the process, but it never feels robotic.
How quickly can you start?
We can usually start sourcing within 24 hours of receiving your job description. First candidates typically arrive within 48 hours.
