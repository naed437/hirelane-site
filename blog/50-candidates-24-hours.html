How We Source 50 Qualified Candidates in 24 Hours

When we tell recruiters we can deliver 50 qualified candidates in 24 hours, the first question is always: "How?"

Fair question. Here's exactly how our AI-powered sourcing pipeline works.

The Old Way (What You're Probably Doing)

Traditional sourcing looks like this:

  1. Open LinkedIn Recruiter
  2. Build a Boolean search string
  3. Scroll through hundreds of profiles
  4. Copy names into a spreadsheet
  5. Guess email addresses (first.last@company.com)
  6. Send outreach one by one
  7. Track responses manually

Time to get 50 candidates: 2-3 days minimum.

And half those guessed emails will bounce anyway.

The HireLane Way

1You Submit a Job Req

Just paste the job description or tell us: "Senior React Developer, SF, 5+ years experience."

Our AI parses out the must-haves, nice-to-haves, and deal-breakers.

2AI Sources from Multiple Channels

We don't just search one database. Our agent searches:

  • GitHub — Find developers by their actual code contributions
  • LinkedIn — Professional profiles and work history
  • Job boards — Active job seekers who match the criteria
  • Company websites — Team pages and about sections

Cross-referencing multiple sources means better data quality.

3Verified Contact Info

We don't guess email patterns. We find verified contact information from:

  • Public GitHub profiles
  • Personal websites and portfolios
  • Conference speaker pages
  • Published articles and papers

Result: 90%+ deliverability vs 45% with guessed patterns.

4AI Qualification

Not every profile that matches keywords is actually qualified. Our AI evaluates:

  • Actual experience (not just titles)
  • Technical depth (from GitHub, blogs, talks)
  • Career trajectory (growing, plateaued, looking to change)
  • Location and remote preferences

You get candidates, not just names.

5Personalized Outreach

Each candidate gets a personalized email that references:

  • Their specific work or projects
  • Why this role is relevant to their background
  • A clear value prop (not generic spam)

Personalization at scale — without the scale effort.

6Delivered to Your Inbox

Within 24 hours, you get:

  • 50 qualified candidate profiles
  • Verified contact information
  • Relevance scores and notes
  • Outreach status tracking

Why This Matters

The math is simple:

  • Old way: 3 days to get 50 candidates → 10 candidates/day
  • HireLane: 50 candidates in 24 hours → 50 candidates/day

That's 5x more pipeline with less effort.

And that pipeline is higher quality because we're not guessing emails or scraping bad data.

What You Do With the Time You Save

Sourcing is necessary but low-value. The high-value work is:

  • Building relationships with hiring managers
  • Coaching candidates through interviews
  • Negotiating offers
  • Growing your client base

Let AI handle the sourcing. Spend your time on what actually makes money.
